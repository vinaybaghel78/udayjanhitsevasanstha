Uday Janhit Seva Sanstha

Uday Janhit Seva Sanstha is a non-profitable trust which is providing support and focus focus on activities in areas involving health, education, infrastructure, advocacy of minority rights and support of the poor.
The vision of Uday Janhit Seva Sanstha is to inspire students with specified knowledge, skills, motivated & passionate about themselves and values to enable them to become good human beings, socially responsible citizens.
